3. Enhancing Rider Experience & Reducing Ride Cancellations (Unstop)

Problem: High ride cancellations after driver acceptance reduce reliability, user trust, and driver earnings.
Approach: Analysed rider and driver journeys, identified key cancellation triggers, and evaluated solutions using impact and feasibility.
Outcome: Prioritized Fast Confirm as the MVP to improve ride completion rates during peak hours with clear success metrics and rollout plan.
